I am originally from China. Now, I live in Columbia, MO, with my dog Croissant. I am a doctoral student in the School of Information Science and Learning Technologies at the University of Missouri-Columbia. I prefer to complete the program by 2023.
I am interested in learning technologies, STEM education, and user experience study for my current research interests.
I hope to work on improving the UX design to help students improve their learning performance with technologies.

I received my Master’s Degree in Elementary Education Science at Missouri State University in Springfield, MO (2017). At the same time, I completed a certificate in Curriculum and Instruction.
After finishing my master’s degree, I worked as a product designer in China for three years. I designed education products for K-12 students and help them learn STEM knowledge outside school.

In my free time, I like hanging out with my friends and find yummy food together. We always do BBQ in the summer.

My favorite quote is:

“Empathy is seeing with the eyes of another, listening with the ears of another and feeling with the heart of another.”
– Alfred Adler

I think the value of empathy is significant. As a designer, we need to understand our users. As a teacher, we need to understand our students.
